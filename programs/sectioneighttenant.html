SECTION 8 TENANT-BASED ASSISTANCE WINSLOW PUBLIC HOUSING DEPARTMENT (subsidized)

www.ci.winslow.az.us/public_housing.htm

Under this program the subsidy is tied to the tenant in the form of a voucher and the voucher follows the tenant whenever the tenant moves to another property. The Section 8 Tenant-Based Assistance Program assigns a program administrator, in this case the City of Winslow Public Housing, to conduct all procedural mandates such as intake process, eligibility and recertification. This program is a partnership between the program administrator (the City of Winslow Public Housing) and the service provider (Sandstone Housing). The rent payment parameters are determined by the program administrator (City of Winslow Public Housing) and it is the property service provider (Sandstone Housing) who makes the determination to accept or refuse each individual tenant voucher and enter into a Section 8 contractual agreement. Winslow Public Housing is responsible for determining that each unit is deemed safe and sanitary by conducting yearly on sight inspections.

Communities Regulated Under This Program

  • Rio Puerco Acres: 38 percent of residents
  • Kayenta Estates: Less than 5 percent of residents
  • Church Rock Estates II: Less than 2 percent of residents
