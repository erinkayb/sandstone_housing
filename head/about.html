About Sandstone Housing

Our Heritage

Sandstone Housing is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Organized in the late 1960s, Sandstone Housing is an Arizona non-profit corporation whose goal was to develop, construct, own, maintain, and operate low-income housing projects on the Navajo Reservation. In the 1990s, Sandstone Housing was reformed as a Navajo corporation with the same goal of helping lower income Navajo people. Now, Sandstone Housing has a total of 664 homes and apartments in 14 developments across the Navajo Nation.
